About Me

Hello, my name is Christine Simko.

I joined the Army Reserves at age 17, have a background in sales and customer service, and grew up primarily shuffled between MT and WYO (but born in NorCal where my family is from). Currently, I reside in breathtaking Marin County, California, having moved from New York City in 2018 where I spent the better part of 10 years.
For a living, I work in customer relationship-building, primarily in the technology industry.

All facts aside--who am I really?

Beyond all societal labels, and things to speak to in an interview, I am generally just a curious individual.
